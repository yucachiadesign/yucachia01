NAME:

Parametric Tower Prototype

2013

DESCRIPTION:

Parametric tower prototype experiment is a project which aims to rethinking the design method. Deconstructing the volume mass into point and curve, we try to read the changing tendency of the geometry. Using multi-agent based system assists us to implement the design concept and testing our design intent. It is tested as a column in a small factory.
