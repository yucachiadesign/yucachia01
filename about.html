Amanda Dox

Architect

Emilio Zhang，architect and press officer of Dear Design Studio. Gained his Master of Architecture in RMIT in Melbourne after spending four years studying Arts in China , Emilio has built his unique innovative designing style.

 

He is experienced in utilising digital 3D modelling into architecture design and he believes that all designs are connected.  He combines his belief and his professional knowledge into his designing projects of architecture design, landscape design and interior design.

 

Emilio commits himself in re-structuring the relationship among architecture, urban planning and landscape and eventually he hopes that his designs  will have the possibility to rebuild our time.

Jiayan Bao

landscape architect

Bachelor of Landscape Architecture, Beijing Forestry University

 

Jiayan has been involved in various types of landscape architecture projects both the private and public sectors. Her diverse past experience ranges from sustainable plants design to modern commercial centers, which has provided her with a solid understanding of landscape itself. The working experience in Architecture office allowed her to understand more about the exploration of Landscape Architecture. Jiayan is passionate about the combination of human and their environment. She also has skills in master planning, concept desgin, plants design and graphic presentations.

 

As a member of DearDesign Studio, her area of focus is small scale open space projects within city environments. She believes in creating more sustainable and engaging spaces according to different types of people, weaving into new urban fabrics.

 

 

Seven Wu

landscape architect

Seven is a recent graduate of Landscape Architecture from RMIT in Melbourne, before it she has 4 years study of landscape in China and half year working experience in a professional landscape design company. She has strong techniques in fast sketch design and hand drawing performance. Her skills included digital 3D modeling, photoshop, illustrator, indesign and so on ,from basic design skills,performance to design development and documentation. Her high artistic literacy make Seven very sensitive in design narrative and performance. Seven is especially interested in urban public space, human interaction in cities and public space design. With a focus on the relationship between human and city environment, she attended community children care center design in Melbourne, explored future urban public space as flexible function area and cooperated with whole team participant several international architecture and landscape architecture competitions, tried to process the higher quality life atmosphere for people through those design. Over 7 years experience in landscape architecture and study with difference excellent senior designer, she has own deep thinking and understanding of landscape and keep making them become reality.

Xingyuan Chen

Landscape architect

Xingyuan is a landscape architect with extensive experience in China and internationally. He is skilled and experienced in the delivery of a broad array of projects across the fields of public domain design, residential landscapes, recreational facilities, natural and rehabilitated landscapes and landscape master planning projects. His extensive history of success executing and managing  landscape architecture projects is borne from his strong artistic, interpersonal and communication skills. Xin Zhang

Landscape architect

Xin Zhang is a designer of Dear Design Studio in Melbourne. She has finished her Bachelor of Landscape Architecture studying in China and now as a  last year Master of Landscape Architecture student currently studying in RMIT University. As a landscape architect, Xin insists that landscape architecture is human centered, poetic qualities of space should be delivered to a place in urban context.

 

Xin used to work with Plasma Studio team as a landscape architecture assistant in Beijing, China. And she took part in previous research and analysis, concept design and previous planning in Izmir University of Economy campus planning project. She has experience working across of a design project including concept design, visualisation, digital and physical modelling.

 

Chu Yue

Landscape architect

Chuyue joined the team of Dear Design Studio in 2017 as Associate Landscape Architect. Prior to joining Dear, Chuyue completed a Master of Landscape Architecture at RMIT University in 2017 and a Bachelor of Art in Landscape Architecture Design at Renmin University in 2014.

 

Since joining us, she has been involved in a wide range of landscape architectural projects including wetland planning, community square and residential landscape. During her university time, she also participated in hoarding design project with Lendlease in Melbourne.

 

Chuyue’s interest lies in public realm design and its atmosphere. So she has a keen interest in Proxemics, the branch of knowledge that deals with the amount of space that people feel it necessary to set between themselves and others. Chuyue is keeping fresh attitude to learn new skills into her future of design.
