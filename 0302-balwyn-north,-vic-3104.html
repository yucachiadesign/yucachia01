DESCRIPTION:

Dalí chose to paint subjects that he considered spiritual, and to reveal hidden powers in them. He believed that all objects possessed this power, and desired to capture it in his painting and his own possession by painting them; this was inspired by a fascination he had with the atomic bomb, which he found works that uses classicism in this way, and is a precursor to other themes that were brought on by this interest in spirituality, such as levitation and the neutralization of gravity.

 

NAME:

TORONTO CANADA WINTER STATION

03

DESCRIPTION:

In this project, we aim to create the feeling of the chaos and gravity. There is a lot of desire, violent and fear in the reality. And they connect to each other closely. These 3 feeling all base at the bottom of our heart just like the structure of installation. The feelings are also controlled unconsciously just like the connection structure rope. All elements compose a triangle body which is the most stable form in the world. Are control and management the key to create a world in peace. We argue that the future should not be a puppet but a more unforeseen result. After a period of time, the temporary installation will be instead of by the new design. It is unforeseen which makes our future more exciting.

 

This project will create an interaction space, which provides nets experience for the visitors through 4 entrances, which stands for the unforeseen feeling.
