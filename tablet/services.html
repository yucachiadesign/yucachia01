PROFILE

We are an inter-disciplinary team of creatives founded by Jiewei Shen. We create architecture, landscape, interior andurban design and we have studios in Melbourne/Australia. There is a distance between digital and reality. We aim to decrease this distance through design and we believe that in order to deal with today’s challenges, architecture can profitably move into a field that has been largely unexplored. Design for fun, design for future.

STUDIO PHILOSOPHY

We consider design as our dear friends and we also believe we can use design to create a utopia for our family and dear friends. As long as we keep doing the new design, we trust we will improve our living space eventually.
